Job Specification

DESIRED SPECIFICATIONS:

GCSE in IT (or BTEC equivalent)
GCSE in Art (or BTEC equivalent)
Level 2 award in Creating a Website
Level 2 Certificate in Interactive Media
Level 2/3 Diploma in Creative iMedia
Level 3 Diploma in Information Technology (Specialist)
Level 3 Diploma in IT User Skills

WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR:

We are looking for someone who is able to work effectively in a team environment as we are in this together,communicate ideas and collaborate with designers. We also are looking for those that are willing to adapt to new technologies and trends in the rapidily evolving field of web development. We are also looking for those that have experience with frameworks and libaries; familiarity with front-end and back-end frameworks like React, Angular, Vue.js Node.js, Django, or Laravel.
